Test a Universal Link

Universal links must be HTTPS. iOS intercepts them when the AASA file at /.well-known/apple-app-site-association matches the URL path.

Universal Link Checklist

  • Domain must be HTTPS with a valid TLS certificate
  • apple-app-site-association hosted at https://yourdomain.com/.well-known/apple-app-site-association
  • App's Associated Domains entitlement includes applinks:yourdomain.com
  • AASA served with Content-Type: application/json (no redirect, no auth)
  • iOS caches AASA — changes take effect after reinstalling the app or waiting up to 24 hours
  • Use "Smart App Banner" (<meta name="apple-itunes-app">) as fallback for browsers
